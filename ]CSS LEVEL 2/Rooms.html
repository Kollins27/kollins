Stay

The Royal is housed in two neighbouring buildings: The Hotel, our classic accommodations exuding warmth and sensuality, and The Annex, which has roomy, Scandi-inspired studios designed for extended stays. We have 43 guest rooms, including adjacent suites perfectly suited for gathering families or groups of friends, as well as a peaceful library, a swimming pool and a private terrace reserved exclusively for our overnight guests.

Meetings & Retreats

Away from distractions and far from the hustle and bustle of the city,
The Royal has a relaxed atmosphere that makes it an ideal escape for
productive meetings and gatherings. Our meeting room seats 14 at its
boardroom table and is equipped with fully integrated A/V capabilities.

Eat & Drink

We have plenty of places for you to eat and drink,
as well as spaces to host your most important get-togethers,
from group dinners to business meetings and retreats.
